TYPING BLOCKS

Typing is a very useful technique that is used in practically every industry. Accurate and
quick typing is a life skill that will benefit people in many aspects. Typing accuracy is
important because it helps to lessen errors in data. In the meantime, speed also helps
in managing time in situations like deadlines or finishing other tasks in less time. Typing
is a frequent activity for many people and needs to be developed for beginners. Typing Blocks is a desktop based game program that needs to be downloaded through a
website to play offline. Students can freely join this game to practice their typing skills.
Its benefits are to improve speed, accuracy, vocabulary, and focus. However, this game can only be played on a computer. To play the game, players had to type the words or symbols written inside the blocks.
